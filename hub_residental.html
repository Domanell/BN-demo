A Flexible Residential Property Management Platform

We build residential property management software on Salesforce that helps property owners, operators, and housing organizations manage leasing, residents, payments, maintenance, and operations in one centralized system. Our platform is flexible, configurable, and designed to support long-term residential management.

Whether you manage one property or a large residential portfolio, we help you simplify operations and gain clear visibility across your communities.

Our residential property management software is designed to support multiple residential models from a single Salesforce-native foundation. Instead of maintaining separate systems for each property type, we configure one platform that adapts to how you operate.

Residential property types we support:

Co-Living

Manage shared living spaces, flexible leases, resident communication, and community operations in one system built for modern co-living environments.

Mobile Home Park

Manage lots, residents, long-term leases, payments, maintenance, and operations for mobile home parks with clear site-level visibility.

Corporate Housing

Manage short- and mid-term corporate housing stays, tenants, billing, and reporting while supporting flexible occupancy models.

Housing Association

Centralize member records, fees, maintenance requests, communications, and governance operations for housing associations and residential communities.

Condominium

Manage condo units, owners, tenants, association fees, maintenance, and shared amenities in a centralized residential management platform.

Multifamily Property

Manage multifamily portfolios by tracking units, residents, leases, payments, maintenance, and reporting across multiple properties.

Senior Living

Support senior living communities by managing residents, units, services, billing, and daily operations while accommodating long-term care workflows.

Student Housing

Manage student units, leasing cycles, roommates, payments, and communication with tools designed for academic-year housing operations.

Serviced Apartment

Manage short- and long-term serviced apartment stays, guest services, billing, and operations in one flexible residential platform.

Single-Family Property

Support senior living communities by managing residents, units, services, billing, and daily operations while accommodating long-term care workflows.

Affordable Housing

Manage student units, leasing cycles, roommates, payments, and communication with tools designed for academic-year housing operations.
