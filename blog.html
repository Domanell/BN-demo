What is a Property Management System (PMS)?

A Property Management System (PMS) is a software application that helps property managers, owners, and operators manage their properties more efficiently. It is designed to streamline various aspects of property management, including reservations, guest check-in and check-out, housekeeping, maintenance, billing, and reporting.
