Redefine the Guest Welcome with Seamless Check-in & Check-out

Move beyond the front desk. Deliver a flawless, automated arrival and departure experience powered by the full capabilities of Salesforce. Reduce manual work for your staff and offer guests the modern, frictionless journey they expect—all from a single, unified platform.

The Challenge: Disconnected Arrivals and Departures

Traditional check-in processes create friction. Long queues, manual data entry, and fragmented systems lead to operational bottlenecks and a poor first impression. For properties managing multiple guests, this inefficiency means lost time, potential for errors, and a service experience that feels outdated. Your team is tied to the desk, and your guest data remains siloed, preventing a truly personalized welcome.

The Solution: A Truly Unified Arrival Experience, Powered by Salesforce

Our Check-in / Check-out Management isn't just a feature; it's a native component of your Salesforce ecosystem. This foundational difference allows you to orchestrate a truly automated and data-driven guest journey from pre-arrival to post-departure.

Empower Guests with Contactless Self-Service

Allow guests to check in, verify their identity, and process payments securely from their own devices, anywhere in the world.

Automate Staff Workflow in Real-Time

As a guest checks out remotely, housekeeping is instantly notified, maintenance can be scheduled, and room inventory is updated across all channels—all through automated Salesforce workflows.

Unify Every Data Point

Because our PMS is on Salesforce, every check-in detail, guest preference, and payment transaction instantly updates the master guest profile. No syncing, no delays, no data silos.

How It Works

Our Check-in / Check-out Management leverages the full power of the Salesforce platform to deliver a seamless, three-step process that competitors simply cannot match.

Automated Pre-Arrival Communication

Customizable emails and SMS messages are automatically triggered from within Salesforce, prompting guests to complete their check-in online via a secure, branded portal.

Secure and Seamless Guest Verification

Guests can complete registration forms, upload identification, and settle payments through our secure gateway. This information is written directly into your Salesforce org, ensuring enterprise-grade security and compliance.

Effortless Check-out and Operational Sync

On departure day, guests receive a link to review their folio and check out with a single click. This action instantly triggers updates across your operational dashboards, from housekeeping to accounting, ensuring your entire team is in perfect alignment.

Key Capabilities That Set You Apart

  • Contactless & Mobile-First Journey: Give guests the modern convenience of managing their arrival and departure entirely from their personal device. Reduce front desk traffic and elevate the guest experience.
  • Group Check-in Coordination: Effortlessly manage arrivals for groups and events. Send out a single link for multiple guests to check in, with each profile and status updating in real-time on a unified dashboard.
  • Real-Time Housekeeping & Staff Notifications: Eliminate communication delays. The moment a guest checks out, housekeeping staff are notified via their PMS Mobile App, accelerating room turnover and improving efficiency.
  • Complete Customization: Leverage the power of the Salesforce platform to customize the entire workflow. Add custom fields, create unique follow-up tasks, and design processes that perfectly match your brand's operational philosophy.
  • Integrated Digital Signatures & Payments: Securely capture e-signatures for registration cards and process pre-authorizations or final payments, with all data and documents stored centrally against the guest record in Salesforce.
