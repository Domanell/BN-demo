Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to implement Booking Ninjas at my property?

Booking Ninjas offers a streamlined onboarding process. Depending on the size and complexity of the property, most hotels can be fully set up in just a few weeks with our 99.9% uptime SLA ensuring reliable service from day one.

Can Booking Ninjas integrate with my existing systems?

Yes, our unified platform integrates with various third-party systems, including CRM systems, accounting software, digital marketing tools, payment gateways, POS, door locks, and check-in kiosks. Our integration marketplace offers dozens of connections with no additional fees.

How secure is my hotel and guest data?

Booking Ninjas is built on Salesforce, which offers top-tier security and compliance, including GDPR standards. Salesforce's enterprise-level security infrastructure protects your data with our 99.9% uptime SLA guarantee.

How can Booking Ninjas help increase revenue?

Our pricing intelligence engine with dynamic daily pricing optimizes rates automatically. Combined with engagement tools, space management solutions for additional revenue streams, and guest marketing features, you can maximize both direct bookings and ancillary revenue.

Does the system support modern guest preferences?

Absolutely. We offer digital key technology, virtual concierge services, mobile-optimized check-in screens, check-in kiosks, and comprehensive guest communication features with branded, customized emails throughout the guest journey.

Are there additional fees for integrations?

No, our unified platform includes integrations through our marketplace with no additional connection fees. Whether connecting guest check-in kiosks, task management solutions, or other hospitality tools, you can expand capabilities without hidden costs.
