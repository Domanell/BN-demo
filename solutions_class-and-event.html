Class & Event Management for Enterprise Hospitality

Break down the operational silos between accommodations and on-site activities. The Class/Attendance feature, built within the secure Booking Ninjas PMS, provides a single, unified platform to manage all classes, events, and gym attendance.

Stop Juggling Disparate Systems. Start Unifying Your Data.

For enterprise hospitality groups, managing ancillary services like fitness classes, wellness workshops, and exclusive events often requires a patchwork of disconnected systems. This fragmentation creates significant challenges:

Incomplete Guest Profiles

Room data is in the PMS, but class attendance and preferences are siloed in a separate application, preventing true personalization.

Revenue Leakage

Manual reconciliation of charges between systems leads to billing errors and lost revenue opportunities.

Security Vulnerabilities

Each additional software vendor introduces a new potential point of failure, increasing your risk profile.

Operational Inefficiency

Staff waste valuable time navigating multiple logins and manually transferring data, detracting from the guest experience.

The Salesforce-Native Solution for Integrated Experience Management

Booking Ninjas' Class/Attendance feature is not an integration; it is a core component of our PMS, built entirely on the Salesforce platform. This provides an unparalleled strategic advantage for your enterprise.

One Guest, One Record

Every class sign-up and check-in is recorded directly on the guest's unified Salesforce profile. This gives your marketing and guest service teams a true 360-degree view to personalize communications and anticipate needs.

Inherit Enterprise-Grade Security

All attendance and scheduling data is protected by Salesforce's world-class, multi-layered security infrastructure, ensuring compliance and peace of mind.

Infinitely Scalable & Customizable

From a single resort's yoga class to a global schedule of executive workshops across hundreds of properties, the system scales flawlessly. Leverage Salesforce's point-and-click tools to customize workflows and reporting to your exact specifications.

Automate for Efficiency

Because it’s Salesforce-native, you can build powerful automations. Automatically send class reminders, follow-up surveys, or trigger marketing journeys based on attendance data—no manual intervention required.

Core Capabilities for Flawless Execution

Translate strategy into seamless operations with a feature set designed for the demands of enterprise hospitality.

Centralized Class & Event Scheduling

Create, manage, and view schedules for all your properties from a single, intuitive interface.

Real-Time Attendance Tracking

Enable quick, digital check-ins for guests and members, eliminating paper rosters and providing instant attendance data.

Unified Billing & Folio Integration

Automatically post charges for classes, no-shows, or membership fees directly to a guest's room folio or a member's account.

Advanced Reporting & Analytics

Leverage the full power of Salesforce Dashboards to analyze class popularity, instructor performance, attendance trends, and ancillary revenue contribution.

Where It Transforms Your Operations

Booking Ninjas' Class & Event Management is designed to integrate seamlessly into a variety of enterprise hospitality environments, empowering you to centralize and optimize guest activities across diverse operational needs including:

Resort Wellness Programs

Yoga, fitness classes, and holistic guest experiences managed in one place.

Hotel Gym Membership & Access

Streamline member management and guest access for hotel fitness centers.

Corporate Event Breakout Sessions

Coordinate and track attendance for workshops and breakout activities.

Exclusive Guest Workshops & Activities

Offer and manage premium experiences for VIPs and special guests.
