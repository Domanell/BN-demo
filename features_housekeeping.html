Is Disconnected Operations Holding You Back?

For many property managers, back-of-house is a chaotic mix of radio calls, paper checklists, and manual updates. This inefficiency leads to critical business challenges:

Delayed Room Turnover

Wasted time between guest checkout and room readiness, directly impacting revenue and creating front desk bottlenecks.

Inconsistent Service

Manual processes lead to missed cleanings, forgotten guest requests, and a tarnished brand reputation.

Reactive Maintenance

Minor issues become major problems because there's no streamlined way to report, assign, and track maintenance tickets.

Zero Visibility

A complete lack of data on team performance, task duration, and operational costs, making it impossible to optimize.

Operational Control

Mobile-First Staff Dashboard

Give your housekeepers and maintenance crew a simple, intuitive mobile interface to view their assigned tasks, checklists, and report issues on the go.

Real-Time Room Status Updates

Eliminate communication delays. As housekeepers update a room's status on their device, it's instantly reflected at the front desk, allowing for earlier check-ins and happier guests.

Automated Task Assignment

Create intelligent rules to automatically assign cleaning tasks based on staff zones or workload, ensuring fair distribution and optimal efficiency.

Integrated Maintenance Ticketing

Empower any staff member or guest to report maintenance issues directly through the system. Track every ticket from creation to resolution, complete with notes, photos, and cost tracking.

Customizable Checklists & Inspections

Powerful Performance Analytics
